# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Codex Lite is used by developers and power users who keep multiple local Codex credentials and need a focused desktop tool for importing accounts, checking quota, switching the active auth file, and inspecting local app state.

## Product Purpose

The product helps users manage local Codex accounts without a hosted backend. Success means the current account, quota status, account type, and risky actions are easy to scan and operate without exposing sensitive credential material.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, local-first. The interface should feel trustworthy and lightweight rather than promotional.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-page ornament, dense enterprise admin tables for the primary account view, decorative glass effects, and invented controls for common account actions.

## Design Principles

- Put operational state first.
- Keep sensitive account details readable but restrained.
- Prefer familiar desktop affordances over novelty.
- Make risky actions deliberate.
- Preserve a compact workflow for repeated account switching.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target readable contrast, keyboard-accessible controls, visible focus states, and reduced-motion-safe transitions.
