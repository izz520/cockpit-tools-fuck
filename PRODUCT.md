# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Codex Lite is used by developers who manage multiple Codex accounts locally. They are usually in a focused desktop workflow and need to import, inspect, switch, and troubleshoot accounts without distraction.

## Product Purpose

Codex Lite provides a lightweight, ad-free desktop account manager for Codex credentials, quota status, account switching, and local diagnostics. Success means users can safely manage accounts with clear state, predictable controls, and no sponsor or promotional surface.

## Brand Personality

Quiet, native, precise. The product should feel like a compact macOS utility: calm under pressure, direct in wording, and careful with sensitive local data.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-page composition, large hero sections, decorative gradients, ad-like sponsor panels, heavy card grids, and loud SaaS dashboard styling. Avoid designs that make credential handling feel playful or casual.

## Design Principles

- Make system state obvious before asking users to act.
- Keep credential workflows calm and reversible.
- Prefer familiar desktop affordances over custom visual novelty.
- Use density where it helps scanning, not decoration.
- Treat errors as recovery paths with concrete next actions.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target strong contrast, keyboard-reachable workflows, clear focus states, and reduced-motion support. Motion should communicate state only and remain usable when disabled.
